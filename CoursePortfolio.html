Design Computing Studio Ⅰ Portfolio

The website is created as part of the course Design Computing Studio Ⅰ assignment. In this course, my team and I completed Project Millionaire Assistants . I independently developed this portfolio website to document the process and reflect on my work for Millionaire Assistant. The website features a clean design to emphasize the key elements. It utilizes a linear browsing structure to guide users during their initial visit. Inspired by Piet Mondrian's Composition with Red Blue and Yellow , I incorporated primary colours as accents on a white page, aiming to bring a sense of modernist simplicity and vibrancy to the website. Physical computing and interaction Design Studio Portfolio

The website is created as part of the course Physical computing and interaction Design Studio Portfolio assignment. In this course, my team and I completed Project Mark Ⅱ-Ⅶ .I independently developed this portfolio website to document the process and reflect on my work for Mark Ⅱ-Ⅶ.The website's style is consistent with the brand identity I designed for our team project, incorporating a blue-purple colour palette and a neon light scattering effect to create a cyberpunk visual identity. It responds to the design theme of the course: The Future Everyday. The entire website embraces the mesmerizing style of cyberpunk while maintaining simplicity to ensure a good reading experience for users.
