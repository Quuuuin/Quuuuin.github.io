Project X

Project X is a fictional sci-fi-themed space exploration project. I am responsible for developing an official website for this grand project to showcase the vastness of space, epic expeditions, and adventurous spirit to users. Users can view detailed information about the project on the website and submit applications.
What I did: UI & UX design, Web develop
Tool used: Figma, VS code, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro
Duration: 16th August - 9th November 2021 as part of Course Introduction to Web Design at UQ
*Due to the COVID-19 lockdown, the project was completed in a remote mode. View the website

The Background

In the course, we are required to design and implement a themed resort website, with the theme being imaginative and unusual locations and activities. The website aims to attract the target audience engagingly and interactively.
Some ideas came up in the topic brainstorming. The first idea that came to my mind is not some futuristic concept. It is about if something happened or not happened, what the world will look like, and the themed resort allows visitors to experience the world that changed. I must confess the idea is inspired by Philip K. Dick's 1962 novel The Man in the High Castle. But I gave up using the idea of imagining a world that would change slightly or be great. It is not breathtaking enough. I need some wow elements. I cast my eye on something grand. As a sci-fi fan, I turn my gaze to boundless space. Searching for an expanse of space is a big dream for some humans.
I learned before that scientists once proposed a one-way crewed mission to Mars at the 1998 International Space Development Conference because sending the ship back is the hardest part of a manned mission to Mars. The above idea inspires me. Since we are hard to make a ship that can fly at the sub-speed of light, why don't we use the time to compensate for speed? That's how the idea came up. A spaceship never backs to the Earth.

Fictional Background Story

ProjectX is a story about a spaceship that condenses the wisdom of all humankind and is equipped with the most advanced aeronautics technology. It is a gigantic spaceship with a complete ecological cycle built inside and the ship has infinite energy. The passengers can live on the ship for their whole life and procreate generation after generation. But the rule is that the ship will ship to the deep space and never return to Earth.
The passengers can explore the universe in their whole life. They will reach the space that human never reach. The ship can provide a similar experience to Earth thanks to the ecosystem built inside. It allows the crew to explore the planets they encounter using small-scale sub-exploration spacecraft.
It is 2077, and the spacecraft is expected to launch in Houston in 2099. The journey will begin with a drive to the Butterfly Cluster M62 & M19 near ANTARES, then turn to the LEO system. Finally, we will head towards the Gamma Disk, a vast system of four galaxies. After completing galactic exploration, the crew are allowed to colonise a habitable star system. Space exploration is a difficult task, so the recommended crew age is 18-70 years, and as we are still 22 years away from take-off, the audience for the website should be 18-50 years old.

Personas

The audiences will be aboard with strong purposes and drives due to the distinctiveness of the resort. The hypothetical target users are mainly scientific researchers and adventurers.
