Safer ATM

Safer ATM is a primary research project based on human-centred design. Through user research in the banking outlet environment, I studied the context and the people within the context to identify problems or design opportunities to improve the existing system. In the project, I employed observation, and interviews, to study how and when people interacted and used technology in the outlet environment. I collected data and used affinity diagrams and sitemap analysis to uncover hidden human values, develop interesting design insights, and identify design opportunities.
What I did: UX research
Tool used: Miro
Duration: 7th March - 28th March 2022 as part of Course Design Thinking at UQ
*Due to the COVID-19 lockdown, the project was completed in a remote mode. View the reserch portfolio
