Responsibility

As a crew member, this ship will be home to you and your descendants.All crew members share a common bond of destiny.
You have to fight for the survival of this ship and contribute your strength.
Keep peace and love alive, and let this small society in space shine a tiny but bright light on human civilisation!

Membership Notice

  • THE SHIP WILL NEVER BACK TO THE EARTH.
  • Before sailing, we invited leading legal experts to draw up a set of spacecraft laws with reference to the laws of various countries. When you board the ship, you are deemed to have known the existence of the laws and voluntarily abide by and accept legal responsibility for responding.
  • We are committed to creating a class equal society. The administrative work of the ship will be co-ordinated and handled by the artificial intelligence AXL6000, which has the highest authority of the ship. The ship's society will operate in a leaderless form (or rather without human leaders).

Application Requirements

  • Have a professional skill. Not just require high technique skill, cooking can be counted.
  • Be prepared to move away from the planet.
  • Be in good psychological condition and free from mental illness.
