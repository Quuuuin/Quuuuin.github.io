Spaceship Structure

The powerhouse is equipped with the most advanced plasma antimatter engine of mankind, with an inexhaustible energy source, capable of providing the forward momentum required by the ship and the energy for the rest of the equipment including the artificial sun, as well as powering the ship's rotation to generate artificial gravity.
The inner ring is divided into administrative areas, functional facilities, Premier Lv.2 residential areas and some key living facilities like Core Laboratory.
Click to learn more
The outer ring carries 80% of the ship's needs, including the ship's natural ecosystem, the three urban areas. The ship's cities are no different from those on Earth. There are all kinds of research facilities, living facilities and everything needed for long voyages and permanent survival.
The bridge connects the different parts of the ship and the kilometre-long portholes allow passengers to experience the boundlessness of space.
In the centre of the three-storey bridge is the dormant cabin for the crew to sleep for long periods of time on their epic journey into space.
